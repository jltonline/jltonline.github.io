Willkommen bei JLTonline!

JLTonline ist die Internetseite der Zeitschrift Journal of Literary Theory (JLT), die seit 2007 im Verlag De Gruyter erscheint. JLT und JLTonline sind der Literaturtheorie gewidmet. Zeitschrift und Internetseite sind richtungsübergreifend und interdisziplinär angelegt und führen internationale literaturtheoretische Debatten zusammen. Das Spektrum der Beiträge, die JLT und JLTonline aufnehmen, reicht von Aufsätzen zu Grundlagenproblemen der Literaturwissenschaften bis hin zu anwendungsorientierten Studien mit systematischen Erkenntnisinteressen. Welcome to JLTonline!

JLTonline is the website of the Journal of Literary Theory (JLT), published by De Gruyter since 2007. JLT and JLTonline are dedicated to literary theory. The journal and website are cross-disciplinary and interdisciplinary and bring together international debates on literary theory. The spectrum of articles in JLT and JLTonline ranges from essays on fundamental problems in literary studies to application-orientated studies with a systematic epistemological interest. More information about the journal can be found on the about us page.

The JLTonline website comprises the following sections:

Under articles you will find the tables of contents, abstracts of all articles and access to selected articles that have appeared in older issues of the journal JLT. Full text links lead to the respective articles on the De Gruyter website. The more recent issues of the JLT are available in open access via this section.

Reviews of publications and reports on conferences of interest to literary theory are published regularly and in open access in the reviews and conference reports sections.

Calls for articles for the journal's issues are published regularly. The section for authors provides information on how to set up and submit articles for the JLT.
